FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How do I connect a new social account?
Go to Platforms → click "Connect another platform" → authorize via OAuth. YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Facebook are all supported.
How does AI content generation work?
Our AI uses your brand voice settings to generate platform-optimized captions, video scripts, YouTube titles/descriptions, and hashtags from a short brief.
Can I schedule posts in advance?
Yes! Use the Scheduler to queue posts (including YouTube uploads) days or weeks ahead, with a visual calendar.
Does SocialFlow support YouTube?
Yes — connect your channel under Platforms to track subscribers, views, watch time, and manage uploads alongside your other social accounts.
What happens if I exceed my AI generation limit?
You'll be prompted to upgrade your plan. Existing scheduled posts and automations continue to run normally.
